## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of Task 3 of the Future Interns Full Stack Web Development Internship.

The objective was to build and deploy a professional website for a real local business to improve its online presence and attract customers.

Cafe O2H’s is a local café in Bangalore. The website was created to enhance visibility, build trust, and provide essential business information to customers.

---

## ❗ Problem Identified

- Limited online presence  
- Customers cannot easily view menu or contact details online  
- Reduced discoverability on search engines  
- Lack of professional digital identity  

---

## 💡 Solution Implemented

I designed and launched a responsive business website using Durable AI Website Builder.

The website includes:

- Home page introducing the café  
- Menu / offerings section  
- About section  
- Contact details  
- Embedded Google Maps location  
- Mobile-friendly layout  

---

## 📈 Business Impact

This website helps Cafe O2H’s:

- Improve brand credibility  
- Increase online visibility  
- Provide easy access to contact & location  
- Attract potential walk-in customers  

---

## 🎤 Pitch Explanation

In today’s digital environment, customers often search online before visiting a café. A professional website helps Cafe O2H’s build trust, showcase offerings, and provide essential information that encourages customer engagement and foot traffic.
